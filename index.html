Q-1.Query the collection "employee" and list all the document?

Ans:-

        
db.EmployeeData.find({})

Q-2.Query to list employees with a salary grater than 30000?

Ans:-

        
db.EmployeeData.find({"salary":{$gt:"30000"}})

Q-3.Query to list employee with experience more than 2 years?

Ans:-

        
db.EmployeeData.find({"overallExp":{$gt:"2"}})

Q-4.Query to list employee graduated after 2015 and with exprence more than 1 year?

Ans:-

        
db.EmployeeData.find({
            "yearGrad":{$gt:"2015"},
            "overallExp":{$gt:"1"}
        })
        

    
Q-5.Query to update the salary of employee with a salary greater than 7000 to 65000?

    
Ans:-

    
        
db.EmployeeData.updataMany(
            {"salary":{$gt:"70000"}},
            {$set:{"salary":"65000"}}
            )
        

    
Q-6.Query to delete all document from "employee" where the last company is Y?

    
Ans:-

    
        
db.EmployeeData.deleteMany({"lastCompany":"Y"})
